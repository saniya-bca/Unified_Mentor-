What is Visual Storytelling?

Visual storytelling means using images, videos, colors, or graphics to explain a message or share an idea. Instead of reading long text, people can understand the story just by looking at the visuals. For example:
A travel blog showing beautiful mountain photos tells the story of adventure.
An ad with a happy family smiling while eating food tells the story of togetherness.
